Setting up and configuring government Wi-Fi

There are two new shared Wi-Fi systems for government buildings. user.wifi and device.wifi.

user.wifi enables users to connect to the internet in any participating building. Users sign up by text, and can verify themselves as a guest inside the building, or a government employee. Find out more about the user journey here.
