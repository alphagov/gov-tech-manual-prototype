Using government Wi-Fi as an employee

There are two new shared Wi-Fi systems in government buildings: user.wifi and device.wifi.

Using user.wifi

user.wifi gives you internet access in all participating buildings. Find out how to sign up here.

Using device.wifi

device.wifi gives you access to the internet and also secure networks like PSN (Public Sector Network). To use it, your device will need to be set up with a certificate by your department's IT team. Once that happens, you'll be able to connect whereever device.wifi is enabled.

device.wifi is currently in beta in the following buildings:

  • Aviation House

Troubleshooting device.wifi

If you can't connect to device.wifi, you should contact the support desk who gave it to you.
